VRDC Data @ Cornell

Experimental QWIPU Schema files

Based on the official LEHD QWIPU Data Schema (downloadable from http://lehd.ces.census.gov/doc/QWIPU_Data_Schema.pdf as of 2014-10-30) and files available in the QWI data directories, this schema describes the format and content of the downloadable QWI files as of R2015Q2. When accessed on the cornell.edu, github.com, or github.io domains, these are not official schemas! Created by Lars Vilhuber (Cornell University). For an overview, and with links to the machine-readable CSV files in this directory, see lehd_public_use_schema.html. A printable version is accessible from that document. To download this entire directory, please go up one level, click the checkbox next to the directory, and choose "download" from the top right menu.
